# Privacy Notice

## English

`X Recorder` records only the content and audio sources you choose on your own computer.

Current behavior:
- The app does not require an online account.
- The app does not upload recordings to a cloud service.
- The app does not include a built-in analytics service.
- Recording files are saved locally to the folder you choose.
- Basic local settings may be stored on the device so the app can remember preferences.

Possible local files:
- Recording output files
- Local settings files
- Local debug / error logs created on the device

User responsibility:
- Make sure you have permission to record the screen, window, audio, or microphone content you capture.
- Do not use the app to violate laws, platform rules, workplace policy, or privacy rights of others.

## 中文

`X Recorder` 只会在你的电脑上录制你主动选择的画面和音频来源。

当前行为：
- 软件不需要在线账号。
- 软件不会自动上传录制文件到云端。
- 软件当前不内置统计分析服务。
- 录制文件会保存在你自己选择的本地文件夹中。
- 软件可能会在本地保存少量设置，用于记住你的偏好。

可能产生的本地文件：
- 录制输出文件
- 本地设置文件
- 本地调试或错误日志

用户责任：
- 请确保你对录制的屏幕、窗口、音频或麦克风内容拥有合法权限。
- 请勿使用本软件侵犯他人隐私、违反平台规则、公司制度或当地法律法规。
